FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is the Odor Genome Protocol?
The Protocol is a 25-point biological diagnostic. We analyze your dermal sensitivity, diet, lifestyle, and sweat output to precisely calculate your unique biochemical needs. We then cross-reference this data against a curated database of high-performance deodorants to find your perfect match, eliminating the guesswork.
Why do most people fail when they switch to natural deodorant?
The failure is a chemistry mismatch. If you have a high load of odor-causing bacteria but choose a formula that is primarily absorbent (like arrowroot) without strong anti-microbial agents (like certain acids or probiotics), it will fail. Our job is to match your microbial environment to the right active ingredient.
Which brands do you typically recommend?
We are an objective analysis tool, not a retailer. Our recommendations come from high-quality, biochemically-sound lines. Brands we frequently match include Salt & Stone (for high-performance), Corpus (for luxury/sensitivity), Malin+Goetz (for a clean feel), and Necessaire (for acid-based control).
I sweat a lot. Can I really go aluminum-free?
Yes. Antiperspirants (aluminum-based) stop wetness by plugging the sweat glands. Deodorants (aluminum-free) stop smell by neutralizing the bacteria. If your primary concern is odor, we can successfully transition you to a natural product with powerful odor-neutralizing compounds like Zinc Ricinoleate or AHAs.
