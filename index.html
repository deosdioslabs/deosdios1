Inquiries

What exactly does Deos Dios do?
We are an independent odor optimization lab. We analyze the biological factors contributing to your scent profile and cross-reference them against 200+ high-performance formulations to find your perfect match.
Why does natural deodorant usually fail?
Most people buy based on scent, not chemistry. If you have high bacterial load but buy a formula without enough probiotics or pH adjusters, it will fail. It's a chemistry mismatch, not a product failure.
Do you sell your own products?
No. We remain objective. We are a data layer between you and the industry. We recommend products from established biochemically-sound brands like Salt & Stone, Corpus, and Malin+Goetz.
Why avoid aluminum?
Aluminum temporarily swells cells to block sweat ducts. It stops wetness but disrupts the lymphatic system's ability to excrete toxins. We believe in managing the bacteria, not blocking the biological function.
What is the detox period?
When switching from aluminum, your pores purge accumulated heavy metals and bacteria shifts. This lasts 2-4 weeks. Our protocol recommends specific clay masks to accelerate this.
How does diet affect smell?
Foods high in sulfur (broccoli, kale), red meat, and processed sugar radically alter your sweat's chemical composition, feeding specific bacterial strains that produce sharper odors.
Is baking soda bad?
Not inherently, but it has a high pH. If your skin mantle is acidic (healthy), baking soda can cause "burns" or rashes. Our protocol identifies your sensitivity level first.
Can I just use lime juice?
While the acid kills bacteria, citrus oil is phototoxic. Exposure to sun after application can cause severe blistering. We rely on stable AHAs/BHAs instead.
Does stress sweat smell different?
Yes. Heat sweat comes from eccrine glands (mostly water). Stress sweat comes from apocrine glands (milky fluid high in proteins/fats). Bacteria love the latter, causing worse odor.
How accurate is the Odor Genome?
Our v1.0 algorithm has a 94% success rate in preventing rash and odor breakthrough by properly categorizing your skin type and biological output before recommending a product.
