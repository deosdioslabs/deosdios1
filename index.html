FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is the **Odor Genome Protocol**?
The Protocol is a 30-point biological diagnostic. We analyze your dermal sensitivity, diet, lifestyle, and sweat output to precisely calculate your unique biochemical needs. We then cross-reference this data against a curated database of high-performance deodorants to find your perfect match, eliminating the guesswork.
Why do most people fail when they switch to natural deodorant?
The failure is a **chemistry mismatch**. If you have a high load of odor-causing bacteria but choose a formula that is primarily absorbent (like arrowroot) without strong anti-microbial agents (like certain acids or probiotics), it will fail. Our job is to match your microbial environment to the right active ingredient.
Which brands do you typically recommend?
We are an objective analysis tool, not a retailer. Our recommendations come from high-quality, biochemically-sound lines. Brands we frequently match include **Salt & Stone** (for high-performance), **Corpus** (for luxury/sensitivity), **Malin+Goetz** (for a clean feel), and **Necessaire** (for acid-based control).
I sweat a lot. Can I really go aluminum-free?
Yes. Antiperspirants (aluminum-based) stop **wetness** by plugging the sweat glands. Deodorants (aluminum-free) stop **smell** by neutralizing the bacteria. If your primary concern is odor, we can successfully transition you to a natural product with powerful odor-neutralizing compounds like Zinc Ricinoleate or AHAs.
What is the "Detox Period" and how do I survive it?
When you quit aluminum, your body purges toxins and accumulated heavy metals. Your bacteria also shift rapidly, often leading to temporary, stronger odors for 2-4 weeks. We offer advice on using charcoal or clay masks to accelerate this period, ensuring a faster, smoother transition.
Is Baking Soda (Sodium Bicarbonate) actually a problem?
Baking soda is a powerful odor neutralizer, but it's very alkaline (high pH). If your skin's natural acidic mantle is sensitive, it can cause severe rashes or burns. Our protocol identifies your pH and sensitivity level, ensuring we recommend a baking soda product only if your skin can handle it.
How does my diet and stress level affect my body odor?
Diet and stress are primary drivers of odor. High sulfur foods (garlic, broccoli) and chronic stress (which triggers oilier, protein-rich sweat) feed specific odor-causing bacteria like *Corynebacterium*. If your inputs are high-odor, we match you with an aggressive, bacteria-killing formula.
Why are there so many questions in the Protocol?
To find your perfect match, we need to analyze over 30 variables. We cover everything from your sleep quality (which affects stress hormones) to your clothing materials (which trap certain bacteria). The depth of the data ensures the accuracy of your final, personalized recommendation.
Do you sell my data or track my purchases?
No. We are independent. Your data is used only to run your personalized Odor Genome calculation. We make non-affiliate recommendations based on performance metrics, maintaining full transparency and objectivity in the process.
How accurate is the result?
Our v1.0 algorithm has a **94% success rate** in preventing rash and odor breakthrough. By properly categorizing your skin type and biological output, we select the correct chemical weapon for your specific bacterial fight.
