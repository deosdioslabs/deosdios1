The science they ignore.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the profiling system?

Our algorithm starts at approximately 65-70% accuracy and improves continuously as we collect feedback data. Factors like genetics (ABCC11 gene), diet, hormones, and lifestyle all play documented roles in body odor, which our questionnaire captures.

Is this service free?

Yes, the profiling and recommendations are completely free. We earn through affiliate partnerships when you purchase recommended products. In the future, we'll offer premium options including custom-formulated deodorants based on aggregated data.

What if the recommendations don't work?

Your feedback helps us improve. If a recommendation doesn't work, return to your profile and we'll provide alternative matches. Every data point makes our algorithm smarter for everyone.

Do you sell deodorants?

Not yet. Currently, we match you with the best existing products on the market. Our long-term vision includes creating premium, custom-formulated deodorants based on the profile data we collect. Join our email list to be notified when we launch.

Is my data private?

We collect anonymous data to improve our matching algorithm. Individual profiles are never sold or shared. We use aggregated, anonymized data to identify patterns and develop future products.

Why does body odor vary so much between people?

Body odor is influenced by genetics (like the ABCC11 gene), diet, hormonal fluctuations, medications, stress levels, and the unique bacterial ecosystem on your skin. This is why one-size-fits-all deodorants fail for so many people.

How long does it take to see results?

The questionnaire takes 8-10 minutes. Results are instant. However, give any new deodorant 1-2 weeks to work with your body chemistry. Natural deodorants in particular may require a 2-4 week adjustment period.

Can I update my profile later?

Yes. Life changes affect body chemistry—new medications, diet changes, hormonal shifts. Retake the protocol anytime to get updated recommendations.
