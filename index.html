FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is the Odor Genome Protocol?
The Protocol is a 30-point biological diagnostic. We analyze your dermal sensitivity, diet, lifestyle, and sweat output to precisely calculate your unique biochemical needs.
Why do most people fail when they switch to natural deodorant?
The failure is a chemistry mismatch. If you have a high load of odor-causing bacteria but choose a formula that is primarily absorbent (like arrowroot) without strong anti-microbial agents, it will fail.
Is Baking Soda (Sodium Bicarbonate) actually a problem?
Baking soda is a powerful odor neutralizer, but it's very alkaline (high pH). If your skin's natural acidic mantle is sensitive, it can cause severe rashes or burns. Our protocol identifies this risk.
