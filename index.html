Common Inquiries

How accurate is the profiling system?
Our algorithm starts at 65-70% accuracy and improves continuously as we collect feedback. Genetics, diet, hormones, and lifestyle all play documented roles in body odor.
Is this service free?
Yes. The profiling and recommendations are completely free. We earn through affiliate partnerships when you purchase recommended products. Future premium options will include custom formulations.
What if the recommendations don't work?
Your feedback helps us improve. If a recommendation doesn't work, return to your profile and we'll provide alternative matches. Every data point makes our algorithm smarter.
Do you sell deodorants?
Not yet. Currently, we match you with the best existing products on the market. Our long-term vision includes creating premium, custom-formulated deodorants based on profile data.
Is my data private?
We collect anonymous data to improve our algorithm. Individual profiles are never sold or shared. We use aggregated, anonymized data to identify patterns.
How long does it take to see results?
The questionnaire takes 8-10 minutes. Results are instant. However, give any new deodorant 1-2 weeks to work with your body chemistry. Natural deodorants may require a 2-4 week adjustment period.
